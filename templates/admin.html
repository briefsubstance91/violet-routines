✦ Editing Routines
  • Edit a routine's name, icon, or time directly in its header
  • Tap ▸ Tasks to expand the task list — click to edit, drag ⠿ to reorder, × to delete
  • Tag tasks with categories (e.g. hygiene, school) using the + tag field — type and press Enter
  • Changes don't save until you hit Save
⚡ How Level Up works
  • When Violet taps ⚡ she picks a category then a win from the list below
  • Add categories that matter to her (School, Kindness, Health…) and fill each with example wins
  • Tap any text to edit it inline, hit + to add, × to remove
🏆 How Milestones work
  • A milestone is a reward Violet earns when her total days completed reaches a number. This count is cumulative and never resets — missing a day doesn't erase progress
  • Set the day count, the reward (what she gets), and optionally a category
  • Rows are auto-sorted by day count when saved

💰 Money & Giving
  • Bonus tasks earn money. A share goes to Violet's Giving Pot for a good cause — set that percentage below
  • Money milestones are rewards Violet unlocks as her total earned grows (e.g. "$25: pick a book"). They show on her Bank page and celebrate when reached
  • Set the dollar amount, the reward, and an optional category. Rows auto-sort by amount

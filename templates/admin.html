⚡ How Level Up works

When Violet taps the ⚡ button in the app, she picks a category and a win to log — something she did even a tiny bit better than before.

  • Add categories that matter to Violet (School, Kindness, Health, etc.)
  • Add wins inside each category as specific examples she can pick from
  • She can also type a custom win — these are just starting prompts
  • Click a category name or win to edit it inline, then hit Save

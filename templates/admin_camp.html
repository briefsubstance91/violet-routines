🏕️ Editing the camp list
  • Group items into sections (Equipment, Clothing…). Set a quantity per item, or leave it blank.
  • Tap ＋ sub on an item to nest parts under it (e.g. Toiletries bag → soap, toothbrush…).
  • Violet checks each item Trunk (packed to take) and Home (packed to bring back) on the camp page.
  • Hit Save when done. Checkmarks she's already made are preserved.

The daily loop
✦Violet has three daily routines (Morning, Afternoon, Evening). She checks off tasks in each one on her phone.
🌟Each day she finishes any routine adds one to her days completed. It's cumulative and never resets — missing a day just pauses progress, it doesn't erase it.
🪙Toonie Tasks are optional extra $2 earners she can do during the week, on top of her routines.
The weekly ritual — Sunday Planning
🗓️Sunday Planning is your weekly sit-down together: review the week, log Level Ups, pay out the Bank, and plan next week's Toonie Tasks. Open it from Sunday Planning.
⚡Level Up is her win log — at Sunday Planning you look back together and record the small personal victories she earned that week, in categories you've set (School, Kindness, Health…).
🏦The Bank holds what she's earned; you hand over the cash (minus her giving share) at Sunday Planning.
Rewards & recognition
🏆Milestones are custom rewards you set in Admin. When Violet's total days completed reaches that number, she sees the reward message.
🏅Badges are earned automatically — for days completed, completing each routine a certain number of times, logging wins, and getting a Triple Crown (all 3 routines in one day).
👑Triple Crown is awarded when Violet completes all three routines on the same day.
Understanding this dashboard
📅Completion % is the all-time average across every routine logged. Each routine is weighted equally.
🏷️Task Categories shows the tag breakdown across all tasks — add tags in Admin → Routines to populate it.
📊Data lives in CSV files on the server. It isn't tied to a specific device — it's server-side and persists across logins.

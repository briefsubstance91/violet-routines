🎁 How Surprises work
  • Violet never sees these until they trigger — that's the magic.
  • Surprise now: tick the box and Save — she gets a push and the reveal pops up next time she opens her app.
  • On a date: pick a day; it reveals (and pushes) on that day.
  • Each surprise reveals once, then shows as Delivered.

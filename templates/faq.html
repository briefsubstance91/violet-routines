💜 For Violet

Everything you need to know about using your app.

Every day you do three short routines — Morning, Afternoon, and Evening. Finishing them earns chores you can do for money, and each week you find tiny ways to make a task you're already good at just 1% better. Little steps add up — that's the whole magic.
🚲 Like in cycling: "it doesn't get easier — you just get better." The goal is never to go back.
Tap a routine card to open it, then tap each task as you finish it (some tasks open into smaller steps). When every task is checked, the routine celebrates all by itself with confetti — there's nothing else to press! 🎉

Each routine has its own time:
☁️ Morning — 6:00–9:00 AM
🌸 Afternoon — 3:30–5:00 PM
🖤 Evening — 5:00–7:00 PM
The app gently points you to the right routine for the time of day:

🔵 Now — this routine's window is open right now. Jump in!
⏰ Time to finish… — the window passed but it's not done yet; you can still catch up.
⏭️ Next up… — the window hasn't opened yet; here's what's coming.
A list of 6 chores you can do for $2 each — an extra way to earn on top of your routines. They unlock when you finish that time's routine (finish your Morning routine in the morning to open them up). It's the same list all day, and ones you've already done stay checked. Each one can be earned once a day.

🗓️ School days: open only during the routine's window — be quick!
🌈 Weekends: once unlocked, they stay open until the next window, so you have more time.
Level Up is making something you already do 1% better — a little neater, a little longer, one notch more grown-up. The goal isn't to do something hard; it's to never go back, so a 1% step should feel easy. Those tiny steps add up over time.

You log your level-ups together each week at Sunday Planning, sorted into categories: 🛁 Self-Care, 🧹 Cleaning & Organization, 🏃 Health & Fitness, 📚 Education & Learning, 🎯 Goal Setting, 💛 Charity & Giving Back, and 🎉 Fun.
💡 Example: "make my bed" → this week, also straighten the pillows → next week, add the throw blanket. Tiny steps, big results.
Money you earn from chores adds up in your Bank. A small slice automatically goes to a giving pot to share with others, and the rest is yours to save. You'll hit fun money milestones along the way, and you can see where the giving goes on the Family Giving page.
Badges 🏅 unlock automatically as you go:

🌟 Days badges — reach 1, 3, 7, 14, 21, 30, or 60 days completed
✨ Routine badges — finish a routine 5 or 20 times
👑 Triple Crown — do all three routines in one day (and on 7 or 30 days total)
⚡ Level Up badges — log 1, 10, or 25 level-ups

Milestones are real-life rewards your family set for reaching certain day-counts (and dollar amounts). Because your day count never resets, you're always building toward the next one. See them all at My Badges.
Sometimes a surprise from your family pops up in the app — a treat, a reward, or a little note. When one's ready, tap it to reveal it! ✨
Nothing is lost! Your "days completed" total stays exactly where it was — it never goes backwards. Real life has off days, and missing one shouldn't erase your hard work. Just pick up again whenever you're ready and your total keeps growing.
💡 Missing a day doesn't undo a thing. You've got this. 💜
📊 For Parents

How to manage, customise, and read Violet's progress.

The app is built on a few ideas: routines done in their time windows; progress that's cumulative and never resets (so a missed day costs nothing); toonie chores as bonus earning once a routine is done; money that auto-splits into giving + savings; and weekly Level Up — the Compound Effect of making proficient tasks 1% better.

The weekly loop: do routines in their windows → finish them to unlock toonie chores → earn money (some to giving, some to bank) → at Sunday Planning, review the week, cash out, and level up a few tasks by 1% for next week.
Edit the tasks, subtasks, icons and order under Admin → Edit Routines. The three routine time windows (Morning, Afternoon, Evening) live with the toonie settings at Admin → Toonie Tasks — the times shown on the routine cards come straight from there.
💡 A routine only counts toward "days completed" when every task is checked.
One shared daily list of $2 chores — extra earning on top of routines. Edit them at Admin → Toonie Tasks (icon, label, $ value).

They unlock when Violet finishes the routine in its window, can be earned once per task per day, and the same list shows all day with done ones checked. On weekdays the list is open only during the window; on weekends it stays open until the next window opens, so she has more of the day.
Level Up is the Compound Effect applied to routines — taking a task Violet's already good at and nudging it ~1% forward. The rule is simply "don't go back," so each step should feel easy.

At Admin → Level Up you can add or rename categories, set icons, and pre-load example "1% level-ups" to spark ideas. The level-ups themselves are logged with Violet each week at Sunday Planning.
💡 Keep examples tiny and doable — the win is consistency, not difficulty.
Earnings split automatically: a set giving percentage goes to the giving pot, the rest to Violet's spendable bank. Set the giving % and the money milestones (e.g. "$25: pick a book") under Admin → Money.

Pay out cash at Sunday Planning (or Admin → Bank of Mom & Dad) — there's an undo. The Family Giving page is where you allocate the giving pot to causes, month by month.
Milestones are real-life rewards Violet earns when her total days completed reaches a number (e.g. "7 days: pick an outfit"). Because the count never resets, they're something she builds toward steadily rather than something she can lose. Edit the day counts and rewards under Admin → Milestones; the Parent Dashboard shows which is next.
Open the Family Calendar and, as a parent, paste your iCloud calendar's public share link (it can start with webcal://). In iOS: Calendar → tap the calendar → Public Calendar → copy the link.

You can view it as a List, Day (a timed hour grid), Week, or Month — tap the switcher at the top.
Surprises (Admin → Surprises): create a reward with a title, icon, message and optional image, then trigger it now or on a date. It reveals once in Violet's app (and can send a push notification).

Events (Admin → Events): add dated or recurring extras (dentist, soccer Tue/Thu, "tidy your room" daily). Mark each as a task (earns money) or an event (just a heads-up) — they show up on the routines page on their day.
Sunday Planning is the once-a-week sit-down with Violet. You: (1) review how the week went, (2) decide what to do next week, (3) figure out how to make it easier and more fun, and (4) pick a few proficient tasks to level up by 1%. You also log the week's level-ups, pay out the bank, and plan next week's toonies.
📊 Parent Dashboard — days completed, completion %, level-up count, per-routine breakdown, this week's chart, recent wins, and the next milestone.

⭐ Stats page — more detail including a full monthly calendar view and Violet's celebration images.
Everything — routine completions, level-ups, badges, streaks, day count and earnings — is saved on a private server, so Violet's progress stays in sync across every device. The parent Admin area is protected by a PIN.

There's no third-party tracking and no analytics. It's just for your family. 💜

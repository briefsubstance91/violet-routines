💜 For Violet

Everything you need to know about using your app.

Tap a routine card (Morning, Afternoon, or Evening) to open it. You'll see a checklist of tasks — tap each one as you finish it. When you've done enough, a Done! button appears at the bottom. Tap it to celebrate and add to your progress. 🎉
💡 You don't have to do every single task — just your best effort counts.
It's the total number of days you've finished at least one routine — and it never resets. The 🌟 number on your home screen shows your total. Even one routine a day adds to it; you don't have to do all three. Miss a day? No problem — your total stays exactly where it was, ready for you to pick back up.
💡 Every day you show up adds one more — it all adds up over time!
Level Up is where your wins get logged — something you did even a tiny bit better than before. It could be anything: being kind to someone, getting into bed on time, or trying a food you don't normally like. You log these together each week at Sunday Planning, looking back on the week.
💡 Small wins count just as much as big ones. Every win is worth celebrating.
Badges unlock automatically as you reach milestones. There are four types:

🌟 Days badges — reach 1, 3, 7, 14, 21, 30, or 60 days completed
✨ Routine badges — complete a specific routine 5 or 20 times
👑 Triple Crown badges — do all three routines in one day (and on 7 or 30 days total)
⚡ Level Up badges — log 1, 10, or 25 wins

When you unlock one, a little pop-up appears. Visit My Badges to see them all.
Triple Crown 👑 is when you complete all three routines — Morning, Afternoon, and Evening — in the same day. It's one of the rarest badges! Each day you pull it off counts toward the badge.
Nothing is lost! Your "days completed" total stays exactly where it was — it never goes backwards. That's the whole idea: real life has off days, and missing one shouldn't erase all your hard work. Just pick up again whenever you're ready and your total keeps growing.
💡 Missing a day doesn't undo a thing. You've got this. 💜
📊 For Parents

How to manage, customise, and read Violet's progress.

Go to Admin → Routines tab. You'll see a card for each routine — click ▸ Tasks to expand it. Click any task text to edit it, drag ⠿ to reorder, hit × to delete, and + Add task to create new ones. Hit Save when done.
Go to Admin → Level Up. You can add categories (like School, Kindness, Health) and populate each one with example wins that Violet can choose from. Tap the emoji to change a category icon, click any win text to edit it, and hit Save.
💡 Tailor the wins to what matters most to Violet right now — they'll feel more real that way.
Milestones are rewards — prizes or experiences Violet earns when her total days completed reaches a number (e.g., "7 days: pick an outfit at H&M"). Because the count never resets, milestones are something she builds toward steadily rather than something she can lose. The Parent Dashboard shows which milestone is next and how many days away it is.

Edit them anytime in Admin → Milestones — set the day count, the reward, and an optional category.
There are two views:

📊 Parent Dashboard — KPIs at a glance: days completed, completion %, level-up wins, routine breakdown, this week's chart, and recent wins.

⭐ Stats page — more detail including a full monthly calendar view and Violet's celebration images.
Each time Violet taps Done! on a routine, it logs a completion to the server with today's date. "Days completed" counts how many distinct days had at least one finished routine — cumulative, with no resets. It's calculated from server data, so it persists across devices and browser clears.
💡 Violet's progress (day count, streaks and badges) is saved to her profile on the server, so it's the same on every device — phone, tablet or computer.
The app runs on a private Railway server — only people with the link can access it. Routine completions, Level Up wins, badges, streaks and day count are all saved to Violet's profile on the server, so her progress stays in sync across every device. The parent Admin area is protected by a PIN.

There's no third-party tracking and no analytics. It's just for your family. 💜

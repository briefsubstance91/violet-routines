💬 How It Works
💜 For Violet

Everything you need to know about using your app.

Tap a routine card (Morning, Afternoon, or Evening) to open it. You'll see a checklist of tasks — tap each one as you finish it. When you've done enough, a Done! button appears at the bottom. Tap it to celebrate and save your streak. 🎉
💡 You don't have to do every single task — just your best effort counts.
A streak is how many days in a row you've completed at least one routine. The 🔥 number on your home screen shows your current streak. Even one routine a day keeps the streak alive — you don't have to do all three.
💡 Try to do at least one routine every day to keep your streak going!
Level Up is where you log a win — something you did even a tiny bit better than before. It could be anything: being kind to someone, getting into bed on time, or trying a food you don't normally like. Tap the ⚡ button in the app to record it.
💡 Small wins count just as much as big ones. Every win is worth celebrating.
Badges unlock automatically as you reach milestones. There are four types:

🔥 Streak badges — build a 3, 7, 14, 21, 30, or 60-day streak
✨ Routine badges — complete a specific routine 5 or 20 times
👑 Triple Crown — do all three routines in one day
⚡ Level Up badges — log 1, 10, or 25 wins

When you unlock one, a little pop-up appears. Visit My Badges to see them all.
Triple Crown 👑 is when you complete all three routines — Morning, Afternoon, and Evening — in the same day. It's one of the rarest badges! Each day you pull it off counts toward the badge.
Your streak resets to zero — but that's okay! Every day is a fresh start. Your badges and total completion history are still saved, and a new streak starts the moment you do a routine again.
💡 Missing one day doesn't erase all the work you've done. You've got this. 💜
📊 For Parents

How to manage, customise, and read Violet's progress.

Go to Admin → Tasks. You'll see a card for each routine (Morning, Afternoon, Evening). Click any task text to edit it, drag the ⠿ handle to reorder, hit × to delete, and + Add task to create new ones. Always click Save when done.
Go to Admin → Level Up. You can add categories (like School, Kindness, Health) and populate each one with example wins that Violet can choose from. Tap the emoji to change a category icon, click any win text to edit it, and hit Save.
💡 Tailor the wins to what matters most to Violet right now — they'll feel more real that way.
Milestones are streak rewards — prizes or experiences Violet earns at specific streak counts (e.g., "7-day streak: pick an outfit at H&M"). The Parent Dashboard shows which milestone is next and how many days away it is.

Milestones are stored in Violet Milestones.csv on the server. To edit them, update that file and redeploy, or ask for help adding an admin UI for it.
There are two views:

📊 Parent Dashboard — KPIs at a glance: streak, completion %, level-up wins, routine breakdown, this week's chart, and recent wins.

⭐ Stats page — more detail including a full monthly calendar view and Violet's celebration images.
Each time Violet taps Done! on a routine, it logs a completion to the server with today's date. A streak counts consecutive days where at least one routine was logged. The streak is calculated from server data — it persists across devices and browser clears.
💡 The streak badge in the app uses localStorage for speed, but the server log is the source of truth for the Stats and Dashboard pages.
The app runs on a private Railway server — only people with the link can access it. Routine completions and Level Up wins are saved to CSV files on the server. Badge progress and streak display are stored in the browser's localStorage on Violet's device.

There are no accounts, no tracking, and no third-party analytics. It's just for your family. 💜
